## Unit 1: Planning in Sports
### Mnemonics
*   **Functions of Management**: **P.O.S.D.C.R.B**
    *   **P**lanning, **O**rganizing, **S**taffing, **D**irecting, **C**ontrolling, **R**eporting, **B**udgeting.
*   **Objectives**: **P.E.C.C** (Performance, Efficiency, Control, Coordination).

### Key Formulas
*   **Knock-out Matches**: N - 1
*   **League Matches (Single)**: N(N-1) / 2
*   **League Matches (Double)**: N(N-1)
*   **Total Byes**: Next Power of 2 - N
*   **Upper Half (Odd N)**: (N + 1) / 2 | **Lower Half**: (N - 1) / 2

## Unit 2: Sports & Nutrition
### Mnemonics
*   **Fat Soluble Vitamins**: **K.E.D.A** (Vit K, E, D, A) - *Stored in fat.*
*   **Macro Minerals**: **C.P.S** (Calcium, Potassium, Sodium, Phosphorus, Magnesium).

### Pitfalls of Dieting
*   **Starvation Mode**: Body slows metabolism to conserve fat.
*   **Skipping Meals**: Causes cravings and overeating later.

### Formulas
*   **BMI**: Weight (kg) / Height (m)²
*   **WHR (Waist-to-Hip)**: Waist / Hip (*Risk: >1.0 Men, >0.85 Women*)

## Unit 3: Yoga & Lifestyle
### Contraindication Rules (When to STOP)
*   **Forward Bends** (e.g., Paschimottanasana): STOP if **Back Pain / Slip Disc / Sciatica**.
*   **Backward Bends** (e.g., Bhujangasana): STOP if **Hernia / Abdominal Surgery**.
*   **Inversions/Intense**: STOP if **High Blood Pressure (BP) / Heart Issues**.
*   **Kneeling** (e.g., Vajrasana): STOP if **Knee Injury / Osteoarthritis**.

### Golden Keyword
*   **Asana Definition**: *"Sthira Sukham Asanam"* (Steady and Comfortable Posture).

## Unit 5: Children & Women in Sports
### Female Athlete Triad
*   **Osteoporosis** (Bone loss) <-> **Amenorrhea** (Missed periods) <-> **Disordered Eating** (Low Energy).

### WHO Guidelines
*   **Children (5-17)**: 60 mins/day (Moderate-Vigorous).
*   **Adults (18-64)**: 150-300 mins/week.

### Postural Deformities
*   **Kyphosis**: Round Upper Back (Hunchback).
*   **Lordosis**: Hollow Lower Back (Swayback).
*   **Scoliosis**: 'S' or 'C' shaped spine.
*   **Knock Knees**: Genu Valgum. | **Bow Legs**: Genu Varum.

## Unit 6: Test & Measurement
### Sports Authority of India (SAI) Tests
*   **Age 5-8**: BMI + Flamingo Balance + Plate Tapping.
*   **Age 9-18**: 50m Speed + 600m Endurance + Sit & Reach + Pushups/Curlups.

### Rikli & Jones (Senior Citizen Fitness)
1.  **Chair Stand**: Lower Body Strength.
2.  **Arm Curl**: Upper Body Strength.
3.  **Chair Sit & Reach**: Lower Body Flexibility.
4.  **Back Scratch**: Upper Body Flexibility.
5.  **8-Foot Up & Go**: Agility/Balance.
6.  **6-Min Walk**: Aerobic Endurance.

## Unit 7: Physiology & Injuries
### Injury Management
*   **Soft Tissue (Sprain/Strain)**: **R.I.C.E.R** (Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation, Referral).
*   **Fractures**: Immobilization.

### Physiology Effects
*   **Short Term**: Heart Rate Increases, Stroke Volume Increases, Temperature Increases.
*   **Long Term**: Hypertrophy (Muscle size), Bradycardia (Resting HR Decreases), Vital Capacity Increases.

## Unit 8: Biomechanics
### Newton's Laws
1.  **Law of Inertia**: A body remains at rest/motion unless acted upon.
2.  **Law of Acceleration**: F = ma.
3.  **Law of Reaction**: Action = Reaction (e.g., Swimming/Walking).

### Levers Mnemonic: F-L-E (1-2-3)
*   **Class 1**: **F**ulcrum in middle (Seesaw, Neck).
*   **Class 2**: **L**oad in middle (Wheelbarrow, Calf Raise).
*   **Class 3**: **E**ffort in middle (Bicep Curl).

### Stability
*   Lower **Center of Gravity (CG)** = Higher Stability.
*   Wider **Base of Support** = Higher Stability.

## Unit 10: Training in Sports
### Training Cycles
*   **Micro**: Shortest (3-10 days).
*   **Meso**: Medium (3-6 weeks).
*   **Macro**: Longest (3-12 months).

### Methods
*   **Strength**: Isometric (Static), Isotonic (Dynamic), Isokinetic (Machines).
*   **Endurance**: Continuous, Interval, Fartlek (Speed Play).
*   **Speed**: Acceleration Runs, Pace Runs.
*   **Flexibility**: **PNF** (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation) - *Best for flexibility.*
