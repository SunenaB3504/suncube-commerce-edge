# SQP Topic Analysis Report

This document outlines the topics that appeared in the CBSE Physical Education Class 12 Sample Question Papers (SQPs) for the years **2023-24**, **2024-25**, and **2025-26**.

## Unit 1: Planning in Sports
*   **Tournaments:** Knock-out fixture calculations (N-1), League formulas (N(N-1)), Cycling method.
*   **Committees:** Roles of Boarding, Publicity, Reception, and Ground/Equipment committees.
*   **Planning:** Objectives and specific planning steps.

## Unit 2: Sports & Nutrition
*   **Nutrients:** Protein for muscle, Vitamins (A, B, C, D, K) and deficiency diseases.
*   **Balanced Diet:** Definition and importance (Pre/During/Post competition).
*   **Weight Control:** BMI calculation, Pitfalls of dieting.

## Unit 3: Yoga & Lifestyle
*   **Diseases:** Obesity (Vajrasana, Hastasana), Diabetes (Bhujangasana), Asthma, Hypertension.
*   **Asana Identification:** Visual questions identifying poses (e.g., Pawanmuktasana, Paschimottanasana).
*   **Prevention:** Role of Yoga in lifestyle disease management.

## Unit 4: Physical Education & Sports for CWSN
*   **Organizations:** Special Olympics (Logo, Motto, Target Group), Paralympics (History, Logo, Years), Deaflympics.
*   **Strategies:** Methods to make physical activities accessible (Equipment modification).
*   **Disability Etiquette:** Professional interaction rules.

## Unit 5: Children & Women in Sports
*   **Postural Deformities:** Knock Knee (Genu Valgum), Bow Legs, Kyphosis, Lordosis, Scoliosis.
*   **Female Athlete Triad:** Osteoporosis, Amenorrhea, Eating Disorders.
*   **Menarche:** Definition and menstrual dysfunction.

## Unit 6: Test & Measurement in Sports
*   **SAI Khelo India Test:** 50m Dash, 600m Run/Walk, Sit & Reach, Partial Curl Up (Procedure & Scoring).
*   **Senior Citizen Test (Rikli & Jones):** Chair Stand, Arm Curl, Back Scratch, 6-min Walk.
*   **General Motor Fitness:** Barrow Three Item Test (Zig-Zag Run, Medicine Ball Put).
*   **Fitness Index:** Harvard Step Test computation.

## Unit 7: Physiology & Injuries in Sports
*   **Physiological Factors:** Determinants of Speed and Strength (Muscle fiber types).
*   **Effects of Exercise:** Short-term (Lactate, HR) vs Long-term (Hypertrophy, Cardiac Output).
*   **Injuries:**
    *   **Soft Tissue:** Abrasion, Contusion, Laceration, Sprain, Strain.
    *   **Bone:** Fractures (Communited, Greenstick, Transverse, Oblique, Impacted).
    *   **Joint:** Dislocation.
*   **First Aid:** PRICER protocol.

## Unit 8: Biomechanics & Sports
*   **Newton's Laws:** 1st constant motion (Inertia), 2nd F=ma (Acceleration), 3rd Action-Reaction - Applications in sports.
*   **Friction:** Sliding vs Rolling, Advantages/Disadvantages (Ice skating, Spikes).
*   **Projectile Motion:** Factors affecting flight (Angle, Velocity, Gravity, Air Resistance).
*   **Equilibrium:** Center of Gravity (CG) and Stability principles.

## Unit 9: Psychology & Sports
*   **Personality:** Big Five Theory (OCEAN), Jung's Classification (Introvert/Extrovert).
*   **Aggression:** Hostile, Instrumental, and Assertive behavior.
*   **Motivation:** Intrinsic vs Extrinsic, Goal Setting, Self-talk.
*   **Exercise Adherence:** Strategies to maintain routine.

## Unit 10: Training in Sports
*   **Strength:** Isometric (Statue), Isotonic (Movement), Isokinetic (Machine) exercises.
*   **Endurance:** Continuous (Long/Slow), Interval (High/Rest), Fartlek (Speed Play).
*   **Speed:** Acceleration runs, Pace runs.
*   **Coordination:** Coupling, Orientation, Reaction abilities.
*   **Training Cycles:** Micro, Meso, Macro cycles.
*   **Circuit Training:** Station planning and benefits.
